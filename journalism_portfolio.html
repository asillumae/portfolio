About Me

I am a sophomore majoring in journalism and political science (concentration in international relations) at the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University. My main interest is political journalism, within the U.S. as well as international politics, and I work fluently in both English and German.
Currently, I'm an assistant campus editor at the Daily Northwestern; in the past, I've been a managing editor and video editor. Over winter quarter 2025 (January-March), I was a national security reporter for the Medill News Service in Washington, D.C., via the Medill on the Hill program. In past summers, I interned at the Pinneberger Tageblatt in northern Germany, where I published several pieces.
